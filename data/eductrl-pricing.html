Frequently Asked Questions Answered

What are the pre-requisites to use EduCtrl Software?

  • If you own a domain name, EduCtrl Software can be setup on the sub-domain(ex: student.yourwebsite.com) or on the main domain(ex:yourwebsite.com).
  • The DNS ('A') record for your domain or subdomain must be added to the IP address provided by us.
  • You must provide an email account with SMTP details for sending email notifications from the software.

  • If you don't own a domain name, EduCtrl Software can be setup on eductrl.app. For ex: if your business name is abcconsultancy, we can setup EduCtrl on abcconsultancy.eductrl.app.

    • Do you charge a setup-fee? How much time is required to setup EduCtrl?

    No, there is no setup fees. Your website will be setup in 1 day.

    Is there any limit on no. of student registrations?

    For EduCtrl Consultancy, there are no limits on student registrations or leads. There are limits on the no. of staff users as mentioned in the pricing details. For EduCtrl Coaching, there are limits on the no. of students as mentioned in the pricing details.

    How secure is my data on your server?

    Data security is an important aspect & we take all measures to secure the server. Even in the database, the critical data like phone no, email id are encrypted, so that even team members who have access to database cannot view the data.

    How do you provide customer support?

    We provide support through support email & whatsapp chat Monday to Friday 10AM - 6PM IST(GMT+530).

    Can I host the software on my server?

    We provide one-time purchase/self-hosted version which can be setup on your server. The license is for single domain. We will do the software setup for you.

    Self-hosted Server Requirements:
    • Cloud Server is preferred. VPS Hosting can also be used
    • Ubuntu Linux OS is preferred
    • php 8.1 or higher
    • MySQL 8
    • ssh, composer, cron jobs
    • php extensions: ioncube, fileinfo, gd
    Recommended Hosting:
    - Digital Ocean Cloud Droplet
    Costs around $18/mth. Choose Droplet with 2GB RAM, 2vCPU, 60GB disk space plan or higher. To get free $200 credit*, use this invite link: www.digitalocean.com
    - Hostinger Hosting
    Costs around $5/mth for first year & $13/mth from 2nd year. Choose 'Business' hosting plan. You will get free emails as well. To get 20% off* on hosting, use this invite link: www.hostinger.com
    - Use our hosting
    Costs $96 per year. 6GB disk space. Cloud server. Emails not included.

    Can EduCtrl software be customized?

    We provide one-time purchase/self-hosted version in which customization can be done.
    For SaaS based version, you can use the settings to configure the system, but customization cannot be done.
